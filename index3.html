Diarise Decorex Joburg 2017 Now!

09 May 2017

DIARISE DECOREX JOBURG 2017 NOW!

Centred around the theme, ‘Make your space your living dream’, Decorex Joburg is set to showcase the city’s premier décor talent and trendsetting design products across seven exhibition halls within the Gallagher Convention Centre from 9 to 13 August.

Co-sponsored by Plascon and Caesarstone, Decorex Joburg, Africa’s premier décor, design and lifestyle exhibition has a range of exciting new features set to meet the diverse tastes of this vibrant city from nuanced tones to overwhelming statement pieces.

Step into this year’s most captivating #livingdream trends in the artfully curated Decorex SA Trend House, or absorb Joburg’s provocative creativity in the stunning Decorex Décor District. Unending inspiration is ensured at the Decorex Designer Spotlight where one of Joburg’s leading interior designers will craft an aspirational home space. And everything you want to know, from landscaping to building your own stools, can be learnt at Builders DIY Theatre where industry leaders will offer insightfully entertaining tips.

An annual highlight on the Decorex calendar is the kaleidoscopic Plascon Colour Forecast where the upcoming 2018 annual tones, destined to adorn all facets of the design and décor realm, will be announced. Experts are on-hand to provide interpretations of these highlighted hues for every #livingdream space.

In addition to these energising trends and décor inspiration, visitors to Decorex Joburg can sample a range of the city’s best cuisine at on-the-go eateries, quaint cafes, fine-dining restaurants and wholesome markets. The venue will also be overflowing with the country’s best vintages and brews at the myriad of bars and pubs throughout.

Don’t miss out on any of the exciting features, cuisine and expert advice at Decorex Joburg. Tickets are sold through www.computicket.co.za and at the door.

Absa Cape Epic – Prologue

20 Mar 2017 Team Meerendal enjoyed a successful day in various categories during the Prologue of the 2015 Absa Cape Epic. The prologue route took the riders along scenic routes through Table Mountain National Park. The highlights of the day include Milena Landtwing and Hielke Elferink of “Team Meerendal Wheeler Cannondale” getting a spot on the podium after finishing 3rd in the Women’s Category. Esther Süss and Alice Pirard of “Team Meerendal Wheeler” finished in fourth to top off an all-round great performance for the Women’s teams. Pre-race favourites Bärti Bucher and Heinz Zörweg finished first in the Grand Masters Category to set the tone for their title challenge in the coming week. Laura Turpijn and Daniel Gathof of “Team Meerendal Rocky Mountain 2” also had a great result by finishing 5th in the Mixed Category. With the Prologue only being the start of the race it is expected that the rest of the Team Meerendal riders will challenge for podium spots over the coming days. Stage 1 on Monday 16 March will see Team Meerendal travel to Oak Valley Wine Estate in Elgin, to tackle 113km of riding and 2800m of climbing which is sure to be a test of endurance and nerves.
